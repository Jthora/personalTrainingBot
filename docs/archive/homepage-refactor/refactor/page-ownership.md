# Page Ownership

## Plan
- Today’s Plan banner (summary, rationale, remaining minutes/count).
- Up Next (single item focus) and limited upcoming list.
- Primary CTA: Start/Resume training. Secondary: Regenerate plan.
- No card dealing, no badges, no profile.

## Cards
- CardProvider, CardTable, CardSlot (timers, hold, share modal, KaTeX).
- Accept cardSlug to focus/replace first slot.
- No schedule CTAs, no progress/badges.

## Progress
- Streak, XP, daily/weekly goals, XP to next level.
- Badges/achievements list; history snapshots if available.
- Informational only; no plan regeneration.

## Coach
- Coach selection, palette preview, motivational feed.
- Alignment status chip; coach tips. Alignment chip lives here (and optionally a small echo in Plan if needed).
- No training start, no card dealing.

## Settings/Profile
- Web3 connect/disconnect, profile prefs.
- Cache/storage toggles, sounds/notifications.
- Link out to full /settings if needed.

## Removed from landing
- Duplicate “what’s next” widgets in sidebar when Plan exists.
- Card dealing and share modals from the default home view.
