# CTA Strategy

## Plan
- Primary: Start/Resume training (routes to /training or Plan focus mode).
- Secondary: Regenerate plan.
- Remove duplicate Start/Next/Mark Done in this view.

## Cards
- Primary: Share card (when slug available) or Next card.
- Secondary: Hold toggle per card; no training CTAs.

## Progress
- Informational; optional link to Achievements or Settings; no primary CTA.

## Coach
- Primary: Change coach (selector); secondary: View coach tips/alignment.

## Settings/Profile
- Primary: Connect/Disconnect Web3; secondary: Edit prefs.

## Duplication to avoid
- Only Plan should present start/resume of training; Training page owns execution controls.
- Up Next lives in Plan; remove parallel “Up Next” elsewhere.
