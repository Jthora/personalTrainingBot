# Page Templates

## Home
- Readiness score + next two actions.
- Offline indicator; sync/preload control.

## Mission Kit List
- Filters (mission type, difficulty), cached badge, deep-link behavior.

## Drill Detail/Run
- Steps, timer/indicators, offline state, abort/success actions.

## Signals
- List with ack/resolve; role tags; offline queue notice.

## Ops/Settings
- Low-data toggle, mute/haptics, privacy note, SW/cache info.

## Acceptance
- Templates specify sections, copy hooks, offline/error states, and telemetry events.
