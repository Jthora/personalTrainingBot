# Goals

## Product Goals
- Deliver persona-correct Psi Operative training experience.
- Improve trust: reliable deep links, offline baseline, privacy clarity.
- Enable coordinated ops via Signals and AAR.

## Technical Goals
- Maintain SPA stability; no regressions in routing/rewrites.
- Ship offline-ready shell with service worker and cache strategy.
- Instrument telemetry for readiness, drills, offline, and team surfaces.

## Success Criteria
- Mission IA visible to 90% of users within rollout window.
- Readiness score renders within target latency (see metrics doc) for p75 sessions.
- Cold offline start succeeds after initial sync for defined routes.
