# Scope and Risks

## In Scope
- IA/copy pivot to mission language.
- Mission kit/drill content and readiness model.
- Offline SW, low-data toggle, deep-link reliability.
- Signals and AAR lightweight surfaces.
- Privacy note and telemetry consent pattern.

## Out of Scope (initial)
- New auth or payments.
- Rich media beyond low-data budgets.
- Complex RBAC; only role tags.

## Risks
- Scope creep across phases.
- Content availability for mission kits/drills.
- Offline regressions due to caching gaps.
- Privacy/legal review delays.

## Mitigations
- Phase gates with explicit criteria.
- Seed with minimal curated packs.
- Automated deep-link/offline test matrix.
- Early privacy note and consent implementation.
