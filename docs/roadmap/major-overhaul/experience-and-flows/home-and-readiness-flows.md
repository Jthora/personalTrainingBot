# Home and Readiness Flows

## States
- Online fresh data; Online stale; Offline with cache; Offline missing cache.

## Steps
- Load readiness score; show confidence; list next two actions.
- Allow sync/preload; show offline indicator.
- Surface Signals summary.

## Acceptance
- Each state has copy, CTA, and telemetry events defined.
- Readiness and actions render from local data when offline.
