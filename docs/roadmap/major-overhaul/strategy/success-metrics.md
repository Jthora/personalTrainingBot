# Success Metrics

## IA/Navigation
- Mission tab reach rate from Home.
- Misnavigation reports trend.

## Readiness
- Readiness render rate; time-to-ready p75/p95.
- Next-action click-through.

## Offline
- Cold offline start success rate after sync.
- Cache hit ratio for kits/drills.
- Deep-link success rate offline/online.

## Drills
- Drill start/completion vs abort ratio.
- Time-on-drill; step drop-off.

## Team/AAR
- Signals item creation and ack rate.
- AAR creation/export rate.

## Quality/Perf
- Bundle size and TTI against budget.
- Error rates (routing, SW update, cache miss).
